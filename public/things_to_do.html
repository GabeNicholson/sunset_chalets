Fox Harb’r Resort & Golf Club

Ocean Sunset Chalets is fortunate to be approximately 30 minutes from Fox Harbor, a luxury community boasting a host of amenities that are accessible to our guests.

Activities and services at Fox Harbor include:

Golf

Enjoy one of the world’s top courses with an option between Championship Course (ranked #43 in Canada) or Oceanside Par 3 Course.

Dining

Fox Harb'r has a choice between casual and upscale dining. Serving fresh seafood and plenty of other gourmet options.

Spa Retreat

The choice is yours: spa treatments, nail services or 5.5 hour packages available.

Other Activities

vineyard tours, horseback riding, sport shooting, kayaking, archery and more!
