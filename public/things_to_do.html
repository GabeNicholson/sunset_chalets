Things to Do

Discover the amazing experiences waiting for you at Ocean Sunset Chalets and the surrounding area.

Beach Adventures

Murray Beach offers warm waters and dramatic tides, perfect for beachcombing and relaxation. Stroll out for more than a kilometer at low tide to search for seashells and enjoy the sandbars.

The warmest waters north of the Carolinas make this an ideal swimming spot during summer months. Pack a picnic and make a day of it!

Outdoor Recreation

Enjoy world-class golf at nearby courses, or try your hand at fishing in the rich waters of the Northumberland Strait. Hiking trails wind through the surrounding countryside, offering stunning views of the landscape.

Our property features badminton courts and outdoor games for the whole family. In the evening, gather around our communal bonfire for marshmallow roasting and storytelling under the stars.

Maritime Adventures

Set sail on a whale watching tour and observe these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat. From May to October, the waters off Nova Scotia are home to several whale species, including humpbacks and minkes.

Fresh seafood is abundant in the region. Visit local fishmongers to purchase the day's catch, or ask us about the best seafood restaurants in the area.

Cultural Experiences

Nova Scotia's rich heritage comes alive in local museums and cultural centers. Learn about the region's Acadian, Mi'kmaq, and Scottish influences through exhibits, demonstrations, and special events.

Don't miss the opportunity to visit nearby wineries, breweries, and distilleries for tastings and tours. The region is becoming known for its quality craft beverages.

Whether you're planning a family vacation, romantic getaway, or solo adventure, Ocean Sunset Chalets offers the perfect base for exploring all that Nova Scotia has to offer.
